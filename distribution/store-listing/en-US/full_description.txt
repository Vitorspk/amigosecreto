Organize your Secret Santa simply, quickly and fun!

Secret Santa is the perfect app for family, friends and office groups. Create as many groups as you want, add participants and draw names in seconds — with custom restrictions so certain people can't draw each other.

KEY FEATURES

🎯 Smart draw
Automatically assigns Secret Santas while respecting restrictions (e.g. couples can't draw each other). No one draws themselves. Results are saved to history.

📱 Share via WhatsApp and SMS
Send each participant's result directly via WhatsApp or SMS, with spoiler protection. The organizer can't see who drew whom.

🛍️ Wish lists
Each participant adds their gift wishes with category, price range and stores. Share your list with your Secret Santa.

👥 Multiple groups
Manage several groups at once — family, work, friends — each with their own participants and settings.

🕐 Draw history
Review past draws with date, time and all assigned pairs.

⚙️ Group settings
Set the event date, location, minimum and maximum gift value, custom rules and more.

💡 AI gift suggestions
Get gift ideas based on your Secret Santa's wish list.

📦 Backup and restore
Export and import your groups as JSON so you never lose your data.

🌙 Dark mode
Interface adapts to the system dark mode with full accessibility support.

🌍 Available in Portuguese, English and Spanish.

Simple, no ads, no sign-up. Everything on your phone.
